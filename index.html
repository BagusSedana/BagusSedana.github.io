Hokkaido, Japan

Explore and enjoy your vacation in Japan!

The island of Hokkaido is located at the northern tip of Japan, near Russia, and has coastlines on the Sea of ​​Japan, Sea of ​​Okhotsk, and the Pacific Ocean. In the center of the island are a number of mountains and volcanic plateaus, and there are coastal plains in all directions. The major cities include Sapporo and Asahikawa in the central region and the port of Hakodate overlooking Honsh.

The jurisdiction of the Hokkaido government includes several small islands, including Rishiri Island, Okushiri Island, and Rebun Island. (According to Japanese calculations, Hokkaido also incorporates several of the Kuril Islands.) Since the status of Hokkaido prefecture is denoted by the name do, it is less commonly referred to as "Hokkaido Prefecture", except when it is necessary to distinguish a government entity from the Island.
